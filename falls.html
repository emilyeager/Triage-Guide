🔢 Category

1: Unresponsive, seizing, BP<80, severely shocked, GCS<9

2: Major multi trauma, very severe pain, GCS<13, severe localised trauma such as amputation or major fracture, chest pain of likely cardiac nature, HR<50, HR>150, hypotensive, haemodynamic compromise

3: Seizure (now alert), altered sensation in limbs, trauma high-risk history, moderate SOB

4: Minor head injury, no LOC, minor limb trauma sprained ankle, possible fracture, uncomplicated laceration, normal vital signs, low/moderate pain

5: Low-risk history and no asymptomatic, minimal pain without high-risk features
